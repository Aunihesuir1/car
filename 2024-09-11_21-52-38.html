Welcome to Bohol Speed Rent a Car

Bohol Speed Rent a Car is your trusted travel partner in Bohol. We have served hundreds of customers who wanted to explore and enjoy the wonderful tourist spots of Bohol. We provide budget-friendly Bohol day tours and Bohol tour packages, as well as car, van and motorbike rental services. Contact us today and let us take care of your vacation in Bohol!

Our Tour Services

Choose from any of our Bohol tour services

Bohol Countryside Tour

Enjoy the fascinating natural beauty that only Bohol can truly offer. Visit the renowned Chocolate Hills, join the Loboc River cruise, meet the cute and cuddly tarsier, or explore the man-made forest. If you haven’t tried our Bohol Countryside tour, then you’ve missed half your life!

Learn More

Bohol Car & Van Rentals Self-Drive or With Driver

Choose from our wide range of cars and motorbikes with our Bohol car rental services. We have been serving the general public since 2011, and you can be assured that our vehicles are the latest models, safe and well-maintained.

Learn More

Island Hopping and Dolphin Watching Tour

Satisfy your longing for the sea with our famous island hopping and dolphin watching tour. Ride on a motorboat and see the whales and dolphins of Bohol with your very eyes. Cap it off with a marine adventure in Balicasag island and the Virgin island.

Learn More

Panglao Island Tour

Explore the natural and cultural attractions of Panglao Island. The island is not only known for its world-class white sand beaches, but also for caves, churches and museums. Try our Panglao Island tour package and see it for yourself.

Learn More

3D/2N Bohol Tour Packages

If you want to enjoy Bohol longer, then choose our  3 days and 2 nights Bohol tour package. We will arrange your hotel accommodation in Bohol and bring you to the island’s best tourist destinations so you won’t be left wanting for more.
